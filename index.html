We have a responsibility to help and to uplift even in the smallest way that we can. --Rodah Ireri

The Lady Mechanic Ke is an initiative aimed at helping women break into the technical field of automitive repair. Until now, this area of specialization has been labeled as a 'mans' domain. We go against all odds to share our expertise with girls too!

Our Focus:
  • Uplifting women into achieving more in the technical field.
  • Encourage equality in skills training and job acquisition.
  • Bridging the gender gap in atutomotive repair and technical career.

Catalizing change!

Why Lady Mechanic Ke?

The Lady Mechanic KeÂ® is a non-governmental organisation founded by women in technical careers in Nairobi Kenya. The organisation was established over the 2020 pandemic period to empower vulnerable girls/women through training in mechanical skills and automotive repair so that they can be self reliant.

At the Lady Mechanic Ke, we value quality work, professionalism and mentorship. Visit us and see the transformation.

By Donating, you are holding a girl's hand on her way to achieving her dreams!
