Rodah Ireri

Automotive repair

Rodah boasts of over 30 years of experience in mechanics and heavy engine maintaince. She has worked with multinationals including DT Dobie and Simba Colt. She has won awards in her field and has featured in many media where she discusses machines and girl empowerment.

My Story


We have a responsibility to help and to uplift even in the smallest way that we can. --Rodah Ireri

The Lady Mechanic Ke is an initiative aimed at helping women break into the technical field of automitive repair. Until now, this area of specialization has been labeled as a mans domain. We go against all odds!

  • Uplifting women into achieving more in the technical field.
  • Encourage equality in skills training and job acquisition.
  • Bridging the gende gap in atutomotive repair career.
